Pantheon: A dataset of globally famous biographies

 

Pantheon is a manually verified dataset of famous people that was created by Yu et al. [1]. It includes 11,341 biographies that have presence in more than 25 languages in Wikipedia by May 2013.

 

 

Included attributes:

 

name                – First- and surname of the person corresponding to the biography 

numlangs         – Number of language editions in which this biography is available on Wikipedia

birthcity           – City of birth connected to the person

birthstate         – US state corresponding to the birthcity (if applicable)

countryName   – Name of the country corresponding to the birthcity

countryCode     – Two-digit country code corresponding to the birthcity

countryCode3   – Three-digit country code corresponding to the birthcity

LAT/LON           – Latitude and longitude corresponding to the birthcity

continentName – Continent corresponding to the birthcity

birthyear          – Year of birth of the notable person

gender             – Gender of the person

occupation       – Occupation of the person

industry/domain – Industry and domain related to the occupation of the person

TotalPageViews – Total of Wikipedia page views on the biography

PageViewsEnglish – Total of page views on English Wikipedia pages of the biography

PageViewsNonEnglish – Total of page views on non-english Wikipedia pages of the biography

L_star               – Effective number of languages (see more information at [1])

HPI                   – Historical Popularity Index (see more information at [1])

 

 

 

Further information:

[1] Yu, A. Z., et al. (2016). Pantheon 1.0, a manually verified dataset of globally famous biographies. Scientific Data 2:150075. doi: 10.1038/sdata.2015.75
